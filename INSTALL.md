# 🚀 Quick Start: Install the App

## What You Get

✅ **Free app** on your phone or computer  
✅ **No App Store required**  
✅ **Works offline** after installation  
✅ **Looks like a native app**  

## 📱 Install on iPhone/iPad

1. Open **Safari** (not Chrome!) and go to: **[your-app-url.vercel.app]**
2. Tap the **Share button** (square with arrow ⬆️)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right
5. Done! The app is on your home screen 🎉

## 📱 Install on Android

1. Open **Chrome** and go to: **[your-app-url.vercel.app]**
2. Tap the **"Install"** banner at the bottom
   - Or tap **⋮ (menu)** → **"Install app"**
3. Tap **"Install"**
4. Done! The app is on your home screen 🎉

## 💻 Install on Computer

### Chrome or Edge:
1. Go to: **[your-app-url.vercel.app]**
2. Look for the **install icon** (➕) in the address bar
3. Click it and select **"Install"**
4. The app opens in its own window!

### Safari (Mac):
1. Go to: **[your-app-url.vercel.app]**
2. Click **Share** → **Add to Dock**

## ✨ What to Expect

Once installed:
- **Icon appears** on your home screen/dock
- **Opens like any app** - no browser interface
- **Works offline** after first visit
- **Updates automatically** when we push changes

## 🎯 That's It!

No App Store, no fees, no waiting for approval. Just install and use!
